Gordon Tipple

Gordon Tipple is a Canadian actor known for his roles in Time Runner, Doctor Who, and Leaving Normal.[1] He also appeared in The X-Files and The Outer Limits.[2] He became the fifth television actor to playthe Master in the television movie Doctor Who.
